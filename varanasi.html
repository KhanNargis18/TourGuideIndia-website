Varanasi Ghats

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

About Varanasi

Varanasi is one of the oldest living cities in the world, known for its spiritual significance and the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremony that takes place every evening on the ghats of the holy river Ganga.

Tour Package Includes

  • Boat ride at Ganga Ghats
  • Visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple
  • Hotel stay and breakfast
  • Local guide for spiritual tour
  • Evening Ganga Aarti experience

Price & Charges

  • Hotel Stay (per night): ₹2,500 - ₹4,000
  • Meals (per day): ₹800 - ₹1,200
  • Guided Tour & Boat Ride: ₹1,000 per person
  • Total Estimated Package: ₹4,300 - ₹6,200 per person (2 days)

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Varanasi, check-in, visit temples, evening Ganga Aarti.

Day 2: Morning boat ride, explore local markets, and departure.
