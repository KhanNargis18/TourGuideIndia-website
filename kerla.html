About Kerala Backwaters

A network of interconnected canals, rivers, and lagoons perfect for houseboat cruises and experiencing tranquil nature.

Tour Package Includes

  • Houseboat cruise in backwaters
  • Village tour and local culture
  • Hotel stay and breakfast
  • Traditional Kerala cuisine on board
  • Evening cultural performance

Price & Charges

  • Houseboat Stay (per night): ₹6,000 - ₹10,000
  • Meals (per day): ₹1,500 - ₹2,500
  • Local Tour & Entry Fees: ₹1,000 per person
  • Total Estimated Package: ₹8,500 - ₹13,500 per person (2 days)

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Kerala, board houseboat, cruise backwaters, enjoy local cuisine.

Day 2: Morning village tour, cultural performance, depart.
