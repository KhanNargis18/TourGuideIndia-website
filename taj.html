Taj Mahal

Agra, Uttar Pradesh

About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum and one of the seven wonders of the world, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan. It's a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of love.

Tour Package Includes

  • Guided tour of Taj Mahal
  • Visit to Agra Fort
  • Hotel stay and breakfast
  • Private transport within Agra
  • Optional sunset photography tour
