About Goa

Goa is famous for its pristine beaches, Portuguese heritage, vibrant nightlife, and delicious seafood cuisine.

Tour Package Includes

  • Beach hopping and water sports
  • Visit to Fort Aguada & Chapora Fort
  • Hotel stay and breakfast
  • Private transport in Goa
  • Evening beach parties & nightlife experience

Price & Charges

  • Meals (per day): ₹1,200 - ₹2,000
  • Water Sports & Entry Fees: ₹1,500 per person
  • Total Estimated Package: ₹6,700 - ₹10,500 per person (3 days)

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Goa, check-in hotel, evening beach walk.

Day 2: Beach hopping, water sports, visit Fort Aguada.

Day 3: Local markets, depart.
